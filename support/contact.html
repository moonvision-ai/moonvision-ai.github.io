Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my personality profile?
To create your personality profile, you'll need to complete our comprehensive personality assessment questionnaire. Go to the "Profile" section in the app and answer the questions about your preferences, behaviors, and goals. Our AI will generate a detailed psychological profile with personalized insights.
Is my personal data secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. Please read our Privacy Policy for detailed information about data protection.
How accurate are the AI psychology insights?
Our AI is trained on extensive psychological knowledge and provides insights based on established psychological principles. However, please remember that these insights are for entertainment and guidance purposes only, and should not replace professional advice for important life decisions.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's app store settings (iOS App Store or Google Play Store). The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
Refund policies are governed by the app store where you made your purchase (Apple App Store or Google Play Store). Please contact the respective app store for refund requests, or reach out to us if you need assistance with the process.
How do I update my profile information?
You can update your profile information in the app settings under "Profile" or "Personal Data." Keep in mind that changing this information will affect all future insights and personality assessments.
Is the app available in multiple languages?
Currently, Auro is available in English. We're working on adding more languages in future updates. Stay tuned for announcements about new language support.
How often is the content updated?
We regularly update our psychological content, including daily insights, mood tracking information, and AI responses. Major app updates with new features are released periodically based on user feedback and psychological research developments.
