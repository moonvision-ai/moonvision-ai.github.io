Getting Started with AstroChat

Creating Your First Birth Chart

Your birth chart is the foundation of your astrological journey. Here's how to create one:

  1. Download and open the AstroChat app
  2. Tap "Create Profile" or "Get Started"
  3. Enter your birth date (month, day, year)
  4. Enter your birth time (as accurate as possible)
  5. Enter your birth location (city, state/province, country)
  6. Tap "Generate Chart" to create your personalized birth chart

Tip: The more accurate your birth time, the more precise your chart will be. If you don't know your exact birth time, you can still create a chart, but some features may be limited.

Understanding Your Dashboard

Once you've created your profile, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

  • Daily Horoscope: Your personalized daily astrological forecast
  • Chart Overview: A visual representation of your birth chart
  • AI Chat: Start a conversation with our AI astrologer
  • Transit Alerts: Important astrological events affecting you
  • Compatibility: Explore relationships with others

Starting Your First AI Conversation

Our AI astrologer is here to answer your questions and provide guidance:

  1. Tap the "Chat" or "Ask AI" button on your dashboard
  2. Type your question or choose from suggested topics
  3. Wait for the AI to analyze your chart and provide insights
  4. Continue the conversation by asking follow-up questions

Example questions to try:

  • "What does my sun sign mean?"
  • "How will this week's transits affect me?"
  • "What should I focus on for personal growth?"

Account & Billing

Managing Your Subscription

AstroChat offers both free and premium features. Here's how to manage your subscription:

Upgrading to Premium:

  1. Open the AstroChat app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Tap "Upgrade to Premium" or "Subscription"
  4. Choose your preferred plan (monthly or yearly)
  5. Complete the purchase through your app store

Canceling Your Subscription:

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > AstroChat > Cancel
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > AstroChat > Cancel

Updating Your Profile Information

To ensure accurate readings, keep your profile information up to date:

  1. Go to Settings or Profile in the app
  2. Tap "Edit Profile" or "Birth Data"
  3. Update your birth date, time, or location as needed
  4. Save your changes

Note: Changing your birth information will affect all future readings and may require regenerating your chart.

Billing and Payment Issues

If you're experiencing billing issues:

  • Check that your payment method is valid and up to date
  • Verify that you have sufficient funds or credit available
  • Contact your app store's customer support for payment-related issues
  • If the problem persists, contact our support team with your order details

Troubleshooting

App won't load or crashes frequently +

Try these solutions in order:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in your app store
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  5. Clear the app's cache (Android) or reinstall the app
  6. Contact support if the issue persists
Birth chart is inaccurate or missing information +

Chart accuracy depends on the precision of your birth information:

  • Verify your birth date, time, and location are correct
  • If you don't know your exact birth time, try to get it from official records
  • Check that your birth location is spelled correctly and recognized by the app
  • Some features require exact birth time and may not work with estimated times
AI responses are slow or not loading +

AI response times can vary based on several factors:

  • Check your internet connection strength
  • Complex questions may take longer to process
  • High server load during peak times may cause delays
  • Try asking simpler, more specific questions
  • If responses consistently fail, contact support
Subscription or payment issues +

For subscription-related problems:

  • Verify your subscription status in your device's app store
  • Check that your payment method is valid and has sufficient funds
  • Ensure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID or Google account
  • Try restoring purchases in the app settings
  • Contact app store support for billing disputes
Notifications not working +

To fix notification issues:

  1. Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings
  2. Verify notification preferences within the AstroChat app
  3. Ensure the app has permission to send notifications
  4. Check your device's Do Not Disturb settings
  5. Try turning notifications off and on again

Privacy & Security

Data Protection and Privacy

We take your privacy seriously and implement strong security measures:

  • All personal data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • We never share your personal information without your consent
  • You can request to view, modify, or delete your data at any time
  • Our privacy practices comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

For detailed information, please read our Privacy Policy.

Account Security

Keep your account secure with these best practices:

  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Don't share your login credentials with others
  • Log out of shared devices after use
  • Report any suspicious account activity immediately
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version

Data Deletion and Account Closure

You have full control over your data:

  1. To delete specific data: Go to Settings > Privacy > Data Management
  2. To close your account: Contact our support team with your request
  3. We will process deletion requests within 30 days
  4. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy

Technical Requirements

System Requirements

Ensure your device meets these minimum requirements for optimal performance:

iOS Requirements:

  • iOS 13.0 or later
  • iPhone 6s or newer
  • iPad (5th generation) or newer
  • At least 100MB of free storage space
  • Internet connection required for most features

Android Requirements:

  • Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later
  • At least 2GB of RAM
  • At least 100MB of free storage space
  • Internet connection required for most features

Internet Connection

AstroChat requires an internet connection for most features:

  • Required for: AI conversations, chart generation, horoscopes, updates
  • Offline access: Previously generated charts and saved reports
  • Recommended: Wi-Fi or strong cellular data connection
  • Data usage: Approximately 5-10MB per session

Supported Languages

AstroChat is currently available in:

  • English (primary language)

We're working on adding more languages in future updates.
